  Hi, I'm an undergraduate in the Department of Physics at Ajou University. Currently studying Electromagnetism and Quantum mechanics. It was interesting that physics and Eastern Buddhist philosophy have common aspects. For example, the principle 一切唯心造, everything depends on the mind, parallels the observer effect. Advances in physics expand our understanding of the universe, realizing unimaginable possibilities. If things once thought to be magic become reality, it would be no different from living in a dream. Listening to music while I go for a walk, it feels like I'm in the movie. It's a single world at the intersection of everything, but the atmosphere of one subset can change anytime. The Many-Worlds Interpretation offers fascinating theme in the artistic fields. Life may be short to do everything, but for one thing. To do one thing, it can be everything.
